# Responsible Use

This project is intended for responsible analysis of public procurement data.

## Do Not Publish Private Working Data

Do not commit or publish:

- real emails;
- real contact datasets;
- private organization lists;
- exported procurement workbooks;
- cache files;
- logs;
- generated outreach workbooks.

The repository includes only synthetic examples.

## Use Contacts Responsibly

Public procurement contacts should be used for targeted and relevant business communication only.

Do not use this tool for spam, bulk unsolicited campaigns, harassment, scraping private sources, or bypassing opt-out requests.

## Keep Auditability

Keep source URLs and confidence labels in internal working files. They make it possible to audit why a contact was selected and whether it is relevant to the intended outreach.

## Review Fallback Contacts

Fallback contacts may come from procurement records unrelated to the target service category. Treat them as lower-confidence records and verify them before use.

## Follow Applicable Rules

Users are responsible for complying with applicable data protection, anti-spam, procurement, and business communication laws in their jurisdiction.
